If you have a European eID you can use that to sign in. Including identity cards, mobile IDs and banking IDs.

Why can I use European eID's?

The eIDAS Regulation is a new law that allows you to use your European electronic identity to access online public services in the UK. This will only apply to European countries that have asked the UK to accept their identities.
